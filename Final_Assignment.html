STEP 6: RECAP - MAJOR FINDINGS + RECOMMANDATIONS ON HOW TO SAVE ENERGY!

Potential Results Perfomed in STEP 5:

  • IT Equipment accounts for almost 10% of the total office consumption.
  • IT Equipment consumes more than the Data Center.
  • The consumption pattern of IT Equipment and the Data Center is less volatile compared to the Main Line.

Interpretation Finding 3:

By looking at the consmption pattern of the IT Equipment in Mauritania, we can see that the consumption variies on the one hand during woring hours, but we obtain quiet a high baseline consumption. That means, the consumptoin during the weekend and the night doesn't differ a lot from the pattern obtained during working hours. That could indicate that UNDP Office Staff doesn't switch off their machines when leaving the office.
